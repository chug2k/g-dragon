Frequently Barked Questions

  • Who are you?

    Hey, we're Chuck and Chris. We're friendly guys who really like dogs. Learn more about us on our about page.

  • What are you doing?

    We're offering on-demand dog care services in the SF Bay Area, because we think that even the best doggy parents could use a little help with their pups sometimes. We couldn't find any dog services that were willing to help out without tons of advance notice. To make it even worse, most people made us feel bad for even asking! It shouldn't be that way. We want to help.

  • What services do you offer?

    Right now we're focusing on solo dog walks, but we'd be happy to talk to you and figure out what you and your pooches need. We offer different lengths of dog walks - from 15 minutes to 3 hours. Our most popular service is the "Busy at Work Special": we'll come pick up your key, take your dog out for 15 minutes, and return your key or leave it in a safe place we agree on. But we also do the normal dog walk thing, with the added bonus that we will only walk one dog at a time.

  • How much does this cost?

    Hmm, the price should be in big letters on the home page, but right now we're running a special rate of $15/hr. Yeah, we get that's really cheap.

  • $15/hr seems too cheap. I am suspicious.

    Hey, we thought dog owners are supposed to be trusting people! Plus, that wasn't really a question.

  • Seriously, how are you offering this for $15/hr?

    The real answer is that we're offering a special rate for our first customers. We're calling it the Product Hunt special. We really want to change dog ownership for the better, but we want to make sure that we're barking up the right tree. (You better bet that pun was intended.) We plan on honoring our Product Hunt special rates for the first few people who sign up as a thank you. But don't worry, we'll always work hard to keep our rates reasonable.

  • Do you have references? Insurance?

    We're still in the process of working out our insurance. While we get that sorted out, we'd be happy to refer to you a few of our happy clients and their owners. Here's an email recap of a recent walk. Ensuring the well-being of dogs is one our most core values; you have our solemn promise that will give your dog the best care possible.

    In case you missed it in the above paragraph, here's an email recap of a recent walk. We're reasonably talented engineers who have written apps downloaded by tens of thousands, so definitely stay tuned - we have some big things planned.

    Right now, we're focusing on San Francisco. We'd still like to hear from you if you're in the East Bay or Peninsula; we should still be able to help. Drop us a line at woof@tryfetchapp.com or use the handy "Request a Fetch!" widget in the bottom right to get in touch with us.

  • My dog is ____ . Can he or she still be a good fit?

    Every dog is special. We'll try our best to accommodate every dog's special needs - get in touch with us.
